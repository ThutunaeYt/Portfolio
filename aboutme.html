Hello! I'm Aryan Baral. I'm a Grade 12 student at St. Xavier's School, currently living in Godawari, Lalitpur. I'm passionate about learning, exploring new ideas, and constantly growing both academically and personally. My house located in Godawari, located in the southern part of Lalitpur District in Nepal, is a serene and picturesque town known for its lush greenery, natural springs, and cultural richness. Nestled at the foothills of the Phulchowki hill, it is famous for the Godawari Botanical Garden and is considered a sacred site for both Hindus and Buddhists. Learn more...


St. Xavier’s School, Godawari, holds a special place in my heart as the place where I spent 10 transformative years of my life. Nestled in the serene greenery of Godawari, the school offered more than just academic knowledge—it shaped my values, character, and lifelong friendships. The Jesuit principles of discipline, compassion, and service were woven into every aspect of our education. From classrooms to playgrounds, every corner of the school carries memories of growth, laughter, and learning that continue to guide me today. Learn more...
